mechanics of character classes

there are many kinds of computational processes. some produce a final definitive result, some improve their result gradually. Some yield results very quickly, some need more time.
  • 🧝 elf: fast & tough.
  • 🧚 fairy: friendly & caring.
  • 🧙 wizard: smart & dangerous.

battles

players battle monsters by casting spells, throwing projectiles or using traps.

elves (tank)

  • they have spells that make them nimble
  • and they're difficult to hit

fairies (healer)

  • they have healing spells and enchantments (buffs)
  • and they can cure detrimental effects

wizards (nuker) ranged dps

  • they're frail in melee combat
  • but they can rain down death from afar
