the game takes place in a virtual world. your character can be one of three classes. you'll visit places. you'll meet characters, such as the ones played by your friends but also non-player characters. you'll write programs in a special language to guide your character.

xor uses utf8 instead of ascii like other similar engines. this means we get to have emojis for graphics.

the engine has simple time, physics and plot mechanics systems. the server is designed for small scale requirements.
